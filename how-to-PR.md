# How to Contribute

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the Awesome World Law Agent repository! This guide will walk you through the process of submitting a pull request (PR) to add new papers, tools, or resources.

## Contribution Guidelines

### What to Contribute

We welcome contributions in the following categories:

1. **Research Papers**: Academic papers related to AI agents in the legal domain, including but not limited to legal reasoning, judgment prediction, contract analysis, compliance, and more.
2. **Tools & Products**: Open-source tools, commercial products, or platforms that leverage AI for legal tasks.
3. **Datasets & Benchmarks**: Legal datasets, corpora, or evaluation benchmarks useful for training or evaluating legal AI systems.
4. **Blog Posts & Articles**: High-quality blog posts, technical reports, or articles that provide insights into legal AI.

### Format

Please follow the existing format when adding entries:

```
- **Paper Title**, Venue/Source Year. [[Paper](URL)] [[Code](URL)] [[Website](URL)]
```

For tools and products:

```
- **Tool Name**: Brief description. [[Website](URL)]
```

### Steps to Submit a PR

1. **Fork** this repository to your GitHub account.
2. **Clone** the forked repository to your local machine.
3. **Create a new branch** for your changes (e.g., `add-new-paper`).
4. **Edit** the `README.md` file, adding your entries to the appropriate section.
5. **Commit** your changes with a clear and descriptive commit message.
6. **Push** your branch to your forked repository.
7. **Open a Pull Request** from your branch to the `main` branch of this repository.

### Review Process

All PRs will be reviewed to ensure:

- The added resource is relevant to AI agents in the legal domain.
- The entry follows the correct format.
- Links are valid and accessible.
- The resource has not already been included.

We aim to review and merge PRs within a few days. Thank you for helping to build this resource!
